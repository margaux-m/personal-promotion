Get personalised social media campaigns

In my opinion, there's no worse feeling for an artist looking for promotion than finding themselves face to face with a marketing page full of packs (XXX $ = XXX Views/Streams). Why am I saying that?

  1. As there's no human interaction (since all you do is to choose for a pack and then proceed to payment), you don't know who you are working with, how these people actually run the campaigns...
  2. There's absolutely no guarantee that the views, streams or followers you'll gain are real. Ultimately, fake views on YouTube are usually deleted by YouTube, fake streams on Spotify can lead to having your song removed from the platform and fake followers can lead to your Facebook / Instagram account being banned.

THIS IS WHY I decided to do it the other way around. Instead of providing a very detailed website full of options, I'm just going to leave you my email adress so we can get in touch in case you're interested!
